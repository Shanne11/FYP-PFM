Class-weighted federated experiment conclusion

Class-weighted loss reduces class collapse across FedAvg, FedProx, and Proposed.

For Proposed, mean Macro F1 increases from 0.043183 to 0.066090, Macro Recall
increases by 0.044653, zero-recall categories decrease by 3, and active predicted
categories increase by 4. Mean accuracy decreases by 0.033507 and Weighted F1
decreases by 0.007956. ECE improves by 0.024351, while Brier score worsens by
0.002729.

The remedy is supported when balanced category performance and Macro F1 are the
primary objectives, but it is not a universal improvement. Class-weighted Proposed
is the leading federated candidate pending paired uncertainty/statistical analysis.
